MailMind Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

MailMind is an AI-powered email assistant that classifies email priority and generates tone-adjusted replies. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you connect your Gmail account.

What We Access

MailMind requests the gmail.compose scope only. This allows us to create email drafts on your behalf. We cannot read, delete, modify, or send your emails.

What We Store

We temporarily store your OAuth access token in server memory solely to facilitate draft creation. This token is never written to a database, never stored on disk, and is lost when the server restarts. We do not store any email content.

What We Never Do

  • We never read your emails
  • We never send emails on your behalf
  • We never store your email content
  • We never sell or share your data with third parties
  • We never access any Gmail data beyond creating drafts

Third Party Services

MailMind uses the Groq API to generate reply suggestions. Only the email subject and body that you manually paste into MailMind are sent to Groq. Your Gmail account credentials and data are never shared with Groq or any other third party.

Data Retention

MailMind does not persist any user data. OAuth tokens are stored in memory only and are cleared on server restart. No email content is ever saved.

Your Rights

You can revoke MailMind's access to your Gmail at any time by visiting Google Account Permissions and removing MailMind.
